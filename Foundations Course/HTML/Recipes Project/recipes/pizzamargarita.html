Margherita Pizza Recipe

Margherita Pizza is a simple yet delicious Italian pizza made with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, fresh basil, and a drizzle of olive oil. It highlights the quality of its ingredients and is a classic favorite.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to a high temperature (around 475°F or 245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add slices of fresh mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Sprinkle with salt and drizzle with olive oil.
  6. Bake in the oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves before serving.
