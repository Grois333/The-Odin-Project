Chicken Alfredo Recipe

Chicken Alfredo is a creamy pasta dish made with fettuccine, grilled chicken, and a rich Alfredo sauce made from butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese. It's a comforting and indulgent meal.

Ingredients:

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Grilled chicken breast
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic

Steps:

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and saut√© garlic.
  3. Add heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted.
  5. Add cooked fettuccine and sliced grilled chicken to the sauce.
  6. Serve hot with extra cheese on top.
